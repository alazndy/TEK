## Core Features:

- Unified Search: Single search bar with categorized results for inventory, sales, and history.
- Product & Stock Management: Manage products and stock using a Google Sheets file as a data source. Supports categories, PDF guides, and mobile barcode scanning. Stock level monitoring and alerts are included.
- Order Management: Creation of sales and purchase orders with order numbers and shipment status tracking.
- Invoice Handling: Upload invoices and link them to respective orders.
- Reporting and Analytics: Generate stock and sales reports with sales price tracking and popular product analysis.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A calming blue (#64B5F6), evoking reliability and efficiency, pivotal for an inventory system.
- Background color: A very light blue (#F0F8FF), creating a clean and unobtrusive backdrop that keeps attention focused on the inventory data.
- Accent color: A muted violet (#9575CD) to subtly highlight important actions and data without overwhelming the user.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font providing a clean, neutral, and highly readable appearance suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Use clear, simple icons to represent inventory functions, focusing on quick recognition.
- Employ a clean and organized layout to improve usability.
