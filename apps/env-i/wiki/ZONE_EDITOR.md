# ENV-I Zone Editor

The Zone Editor is a powerful tool for designing warehouse layouts visually. It helps in mapping physical locations (Zones and Shelves) to the digital inventory system.

## Key Features

- **Visual Floor Plan**: Drag-and-drop interface to position zones on a 100x100 grid.
- **Zone Management**: Create, resize, rename, and color-code zones.
- **Shelf Configuration**: Add multiple shelves to each zone with custom row and column counts.
- **Collision Detection**: Prevents zones from overlapping to ensure a clean layout.
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and tablet for on-site configuration.

## How to Use

### Accessing the Editor

Navigate to **Warehouse Settings** > **Map Editor**.

### Creating a Zone

1. Click the **"Bölge Ekle"** (Add Zone) button.
2. A new zone appears on the grid.
3. Click the zone to select it.

### Modifying a Zone

- **Move**: Click and drag the zone body.
- **Resize**: Click and drag the handle at the bottom-right corner.
- **Properties**: Use the right-side panel to adjust names, colors, and precise dimensions.

### Managing Shelves

1. Select a zone.
2. In the properties panel, find the "Raflar" (Shelves) section.
3. Click **"Raf Ekle"** to add a new shelf unit.
4. Define the storage capacity (Rows x Columns) for that shelf.

### Saving

Click **"Planı Kaydet"** to persist changes to the database.
