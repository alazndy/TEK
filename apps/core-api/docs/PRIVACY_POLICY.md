# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2025-01-01

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to T-Ecosystem. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering at the Services, specifically:

- Name and Email Address.
- Profile information (Avatar, Roles).
- Usage data (Projects, Inventory interactions).

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- To facilitate account creation and logon process.
- To send administrative information to you.
- To protect our Services (Security monitoring, Audit logs).

## 4. Your Rights (GDPR/KVKK)

You have certain rights under applicable data protection laws:

- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of your personal data.
- **Right to Rectification**: Request correction of inaccurate data.
- **Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")**: You can delete your account at any time via the User Profile DELETE action (Endpoint: `DELETE /users/me`).
- **Right to Data Portability**: Request transfer of your data.

## 5. Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process (WAF, Encryption, RBAC).

## 6. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, you may email us at [privacy@t-ecosystem.com].
